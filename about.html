Built for the part of the week that's actually yours.

Weekender is a golf brand for people who play for the love of it — not the dress code. We're here for the early tee time with your friends, the back nine that runs long, the round that turns into dinner.

Our story

The weekend, defended.

Somewhere along the way, golf got corporate. Stiff, repetitive, a little joyless — built for the clubhouse, not for the people actually out playing.

We started Weekender in Northwest Arkansas to make the opposite of that: apparel that feels like your favorite Saturday with your favorite people. Call it a quiet protest against the nine-to-five, worn on the course. The weekend is the point.

The product

Made in limited runs, built to last.

We don't do endless racks. Weekender arrives in small, intentional drops — starting with three performance polos held to an obsessive standard: an elevated athletic fit, breathable performance fabric, and branding subtle enough to wear anywhere.

Premium, between $99 and $119, and made to look right well past the season. When a drop is gone, it's gone.

The bigger idea

More than a shirt.
